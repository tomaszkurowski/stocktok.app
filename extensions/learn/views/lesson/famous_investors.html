Famous Investors

Learnings from others (especially investing gurus) are always most valuable lessons. In this lesson you can read six biographies from Hall-of-Fame of most successfull investors.

Warren Buffet

CEO of Berkshire Hathaway, Oracle of Omaha, Most successful investor in the world

Charlie Munger

Vice-President of Berkshire Hathaway, Closest Business-Partner of W.Buffet, Value Investor

George Soros

Most successful Hungarian Investor, Man Who Broke Bank of England, Businessman & Filantropist

Milton Friedman

Nobel-Awarded American economist and statistication, Inventor of significant Economy theories

Michael Burry

American Investor & hedge fund manager, Famous Big-Shorter on subprime mortage crisis in 2007

Peter Lynch

Investor & mutual fund manager, Author of investing strategic books, Proponent of value investing
