What is Stocktok?

Stocktok is virtual financial manager. You can find assets, manage them in wallet and compete with other players in rankings.

Its not based on real cash. Every player is getting virtual 25k $ of funds to play. Than, like in real market, goal is to get as much as it is possible from that.

You can play with different strategies, with different leverages and with advanced wallet approach, in which all assets work together.

If you are not interested in playing, You can switch your wallet to "silent". Which will not be visible for others. Still it can keep assets.

How to play?

Observe

If you've never read about markets & investing, than probably the best way to start is just to observe. You can find in stocks, currencies, commodities, indicies, crypto, tokens. Add them to observed and start some research on a daily basis.

Learn

There are plenty possible strategies. Warrent Buffet's longterm value, technical/graph analysis, price vs speculations and many many more. No matter of which strategy you will follow naturally, you will need research.

Try

Try to invest (virtually) in prefered strategy into your choices. And keep observing and learning. If your wallet will be strong, you can compete with other players in rankings.
